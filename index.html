Fade Barber Shop

At Fade Barber Shop in the heart of Limassol, Cyprus, we believe a place that offers a variety of services of the highest quality, while maintaining the classic feel of a gentlemen's shop, is the perfect balance. So we assembled Limassol’s finest barbers and established a barber shop in a busy, easy accessible city centre of Limassol.
With over 7 years of experience, each one of our barbers strives to help our clients stay sharp. Our attention is on the details, so our clients will always leave looking clean and fresh. No matter whether you’re visiting us for the first time or have been with us for years, you will get high quality services with pleasurable results.
